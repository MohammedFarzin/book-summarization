**Summary of The Magic of Thinking Big by David Schwartz (First Two Chapters)**

**5 Core Concepts:**

1. Believe you can succeed and you will.
2. Cure yourself of excusitis, the failure disease.
3. Build confidence and destroy fear.
4. How you think determines how you act.
5. Think right toward people.

**Breakthrough Ideas:**

1. The size of your success is determined by the size of your belief.
2. You are not a product of your environment, but a product of your decisions.
3. Fear is a state of mind, and you can eliminate it by changing your thinking.

**Quotable Insight:**
"Thinking big is the key which unlocks the door of opportunity."

**Practical Applications:**

1. Set high goals and believe you can achieve them.
2. Identify and challenge negative thoughts and beliefs.
3. Practice visualization and positive affirmations.
4. Treat others with respect and kindness.

**Why It's a Must-Read:**
The Magic of Thinking Big provides practical and actionable advice for improving one's mindset and achieving success. It challenges readers to think bigger and overcome limiting beliefs.

**For Readers Who Enjoyed:**

1. Think and Grow Rich by Napoleon Hill
2. The Power of Positive Thinking by Norman Vincent Peale

**Final Thought:**
The Magic of Thinking Big encourages readers to think positively, set high goals, and take action towards achieving success. By changing the way you think, you can change the way you live.
