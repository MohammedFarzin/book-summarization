**Summary of The Magic of Thinking Big by David Schwartz**

**5 Core Concepts:**

- Believe you can succeed: Your mindset determines your success. Believe in your potential and capabilities.
- Cure yourself of excusitis: Stop making excuses and take responsibility for your actions and decisions.
- Think big: Set high goals and think creatively to achieve them.
- Manage your attitude: Your attitude affects your actions and interactions. Choose a positive attitude.
- Use your brainpower: Develop and use your thinking skills to solve problems and make decisions.

**Breakthrough Ideas:**

- The size of your success is determined by the size of your thinking.
- Your attitude is a choice, and it affects your actions and interactions.
- You can develop and improve your thinking skills.

**Quotable Insight:**

- "Thinking big is the key to unlocking your potential and achieving success."

**Practical Applications:**

- Set high goals and think creatively to achieve them.
- Take responsibility for your actions and decisions.
- Choose a positive attitude and manage it daily.
- Develop and use your thinking skills to solve problems and make decisions.

**Why It's a Must-Read:**

- It provides practical strategies for achieving success and reaching your potential.
- It challenges readers to think big and set high goals.
- It encourages readers to take responsibility for their actions and decisions.
